## PRICING PSYCHOLOGY

### Anchoring Strategy

On pricing page, show:
1. Enterprise tier first (R5,999) - sets anchor
2. "Most Popular" on Growth (R1,499) - guides choice
3. Starter (R699) - entry point

### Free Tier Strategy

```
FREE TIER PURPOSE:
├── B2C: 1 free CV → Creates account → Email nurture → Upsell
├── B2B: 5 free CVs → Proves value → Sales follow-up → Convert
└── B2R: 10 free CVs → Demonstrates ROI → High-touch sales → Close
```

### Annual Discount

- 20% off for annual payment

- Reduces churn
- Standard SaaS practice

### Enterprise Custom Pricing

For 50+ employees or 10+ recruiters:
- Custom pricing conversation
- Volume discounts available
- Implementation support included
- This signals "serious business"

## WHY THESE PRICES WORK

### For B2C (R79-R349)
- Below "impulse buy" threshold
- More than "throwaway" (perceived value)
- Comparable to career coaching session (R500+)
- 0.07% of a R500K salary

### For B2B (R699-R5,999)
- Starter = less than one recruiter hour/week
- Growth = less than a job ad on LinkedIn
- Scale = less than one recruitment agency fee
- Enterprise = still 10x cheaper than hiring an extra HR person

### For B2Recruiter (R1,499-R14,999)
- Solo = one placement fee covers 4 years
- Agency = 0.1% of annual revenue for 20% productivity gain
- Enterprise = serious tool for serious agencies

## DISCOUNTING POLICY

### When to Discount
- Annual commitment: 20% off
- Non-profit/NGO: 50% off
- Startup (<2 years): 30% off first year
- Referral: 1 month free for both parties

### When NOT to Discount
- "Can we get a lower price?" - No, but can do annual
- Enterprise without proper qualification
- Anyone who doesn't see value

**Never compete on price. Compete on value.**
