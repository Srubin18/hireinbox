## UNDERSTANDING THE RECRUITER

### Their Business Model
```
Recruiter gets paid ONLY when placement happens.

Fee = 15-25% of candidate's annual salary
Average SA placement fee = R50,000 - R150,000
Average time to fill = 30-60 days
Average recruiter handles = 10-20 roles at once

THEIR PROBLEM: Every hour NOT placing = money lost
```

### Their Daily Pain Points

| Pain | Time Wasted | Impact |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Reading CVs | 2-3 hours/day | Low value work |
| Writing candidate summaries | 30 min/candidate | Boring, repetitive |
| Formatting CVs for clients | 20 min/candidate | No value add |
| Scheduling interviews | 1 hour/day | Admin hell |
| Chasing candidates | 1 hour/day | Frustrating |
| Client feedback delays | Days/weeks | Kills momentum |
| Candidate no-shows | 20% of interviews | Embarrassing |
| Wrong candidates sent | 30% rejection rate | Client trust lost |
