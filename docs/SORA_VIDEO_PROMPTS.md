# SORA AI Video Prompts - Creator Passport Campaign

## MAIN VIDEO (60 seconds)

### Scene 1: The Rejection (0-8s)
```
A young South African content creator sits at a desk, looking frustrated at their laptop.
Email notifications pop up on screen showing "Application Declined" and "Not Selected".
The lighting is moody, slightly dark. They slump back in their chair, defeated.
Close-up on their face showing disappointment. Modern apartment in Johannesburg or Cape Town.
Cinematic, 4K, shallow depth of field, natural lighting from window.
```

### Scene 2: The Fraud Headlines (8-15s)
```

"Influencer Fraud Costs Brands $2 Billion"
"55% of Instagram Engagement is Fake"
"1 in 4 Influencers Buy Followers"
Headlines zoom and rotate, glitch effects, red warning colors.
```

### Scene 3: The Frustration (15-22s)
```
The same creator throws their hands up in frustration, talking directly to camera.
They gesture at their phone showing their real content and engagement.
Text overlay appears: "YOU'RE REAL. BUT YOU LOOK FAKE."
Handheld camera movement, authentic documentary style.
Natural lighting, casual home office setting.
```

### Scene 4: The Transformation (22-30s)
```
Transition effect: dark room brightens, sun comes through window.
Creator sits up straighter, looks at phone with new hope.
Screen shows "Creator Passport" interface loading.
Mood shift from frustration to curiosity to excitement.
Color grade shifts from cool/blue to warm/golden.
Smooth camera push-in on their face.
```

### Scene 5: The Passport Demo (30-45s)
```
Over-the-shoulder shot of phone screen showing Creator Passport interface.
Video analysis playing - scores appearing: Authenticity 87, Confidence 82, Engagement 91.
Timeline visualization showing their video journey.
Green checkmark animation: "VERIFIED CREATOR"
Clean UI, purple and white brand colors, modern tech aesthetic.
Smooth screen recording style with subtle 3D depth.
```

### Scene 6: The Win (45-55s)
```
Creator's phone buzzes. DM notification: "Hi! We'd love to work with you!"
Their face lights up with genuine excitement.
Quick montage: handshake, signing contract, product unboxing, creating content.
Split screen: before (rejected) vs after (verified and working).
Upbeat energy, bright lighting, success vibes.
```

### Scene 7: Call to Action (55-60s)
```
Creator looks directly at camera, confident smile.
Text overlay: "R49. 2 MINUTES. LINK IN BIO."
QR code appears on screen.
HireInbox logo fade in.
Clean white/purple background, professional lighting.
Hold on confident face for 2 seconds.
```

## STYLE GUIDE FOR SORA

### Color Palette
- Primary: Purple (#4F46E5) - HireInbox brand
- Secondary: White (#FFFFFF)
- Accent: Green (#10B981) - verification/success
- Warning: Red (#EF4444) - fraud/rejection

### Mood Progression
1. Opening: Dark, frustrated, moody
2. Middle: Transition, hope emerging
3. End: Bright, confident, successful

### Camera Style
- Handheld for authentic moments
- Smooth gimbal for product shots
- Quick cuts (2-3 seconds)
- Face close-ups for emotion

### Text Style
- Bold, sans-serif font
- High contrast against background
- Animate in with impact
- Always readable on mobile

### Talent
- South African creators
- Diverse representation
- Age 20-35
- Authentic, relatable, not polished influencer type
