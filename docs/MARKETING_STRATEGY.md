# HireInbox Marketing Strategy - South Africa Launch

**Budget:** R150,000/month
**Split:** 90% B2B (R135,000) | 10% B2C (R15,000)
**Target Market:** SMEs, Recruitment Agencies, HR Teams
**Research Date:** January 2026

---

## Executive Summary

This marketing strategy outlines the optimal channel mix for HireInbox's South African launch. Based on current market research, we recommend a diversified approach leveraging LinkedIn's strong B2B performance, Google Ads for high-intent search capture, and cost-effective social channels for B2C job seeker acquisition.

**Key Findings:**
- South Africa offers significantly lower digital advertising costs than global averages (Facebook CPM ~86% below global)
- LinkedIn delivers 113% ROI for B2B vs Google's 78%, with 28% lower CPL
- WhatsApp has 93.8% active user engagement in SA - critical for B2C
- B2B SaaS CAC in emerging markets can be 2-5x cheaper than US/EU markets

### 3.2 Conversion Funnel Assumptions

**B2B Funnel:**

```
Website Visitors    →    Leads (MQL)    →    Qualified (SQL)    →    Demo    →    Customer
    100%                   3-5%              50-60%               40-50%        20-30%
```

| Stage | Conversion Rate | From 10,000 visitors |
|-------|-----------------|----------------------|
| Visitor → Lead | 3-5% | 300-500 leads |
| Lead → SQL | 50-60% | 150-300 SQLs |
| SQL → Demo | 40-50% | 60-150 demos |
| Demo → Customer | 20-30% | 12-45 customers |

**Blended B2B Metrics:**
- Overall visitor → customer: 0.12-0.45%
- Target CAC: R5,000-R15,000 per customer
- LTV:CAC target: 3:1 minimum

**B2C Funnel:**

```
Impression    →    Click    →    Install    →    Active User    →    Premium (future)
  100%             1-3%          20-40%          30-50%             5-10%
```
