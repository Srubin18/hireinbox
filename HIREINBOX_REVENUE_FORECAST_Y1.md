## ASSUMPTIONS

### Market Assumptions
- South Africa has ~2.5 million SMEs
- ~500,000 hire at least once per year
- Average SME posts 3-5 roles per year
- Entry-level/mid-market focus (not enterprise)

### Growth Assumptions
- Month 1-3: Heavy marketing push, steep learning curve
- Month 4-6: Product-market fit, word of mouth begins
- Month 7-12: Accelerating growth, referrals compound

## WHAT DRIVES THESE NUMBERS

### B2B Growth Drivers
1. HR association partnerships (SABPP, IPM)
2. Recruitment agency bulk deals
3. Word-of-mouth from happy customers
4. Case study marketing
5. LinkedIn thought leadership

### B2C Growth Drivers
1. SEO for "CV help South Africa"
2. University career center partnerships
3. WhatsApp sharing of results
4. Social proof (testimonials)
5. Low price point reduces friction

### Talent Pool Growth Drivers
1. Network effects (more candidates = more employers)
2. Job seeker marketing drives candidate supply
3. Successful placements create word-of-mouth
4. Employer success stories
